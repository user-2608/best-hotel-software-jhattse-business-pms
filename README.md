# **Jhattse Business – Hotel Management Software**

Jhattse Business Hotel Software is presented as an all-in-one hotel management platform designed to simplify daily operations and enhance guest experiences. It brings essential hotel functions—front desk, reservations, restaurant operations, housekeeping, payments, and communication—into a single integrated system for better control and smoother workflows.

---

## **Key Features**

### **1. Property Management System (PMS)**

A centralized front-desk interface to manage:

* Check-in & check-out
* Guest profiles and stay history
* Room assignment & availability
* Reservation tracking
* Front desk operations
  This module is designed to help staff work efficiently while maintaining accurate guest records.

### **2. Channel Manager**

A real-time distribution tool intended to sync:

* Rates
* Availability
* Inventory
  across connected OTAs and booking platforms. This may help reduce overbookings and maintain consistent pricing.

### **3. Restaurant POS**

A point-of-sale module for hotel restaurants and cafés:

* Order taking
* KOT/BOT management
* Table and billing operations
* Room posting for in-house guests
  This feature is aimed at providing a smoother dining experience.

### **4. WhatsApp Business Integration**

Communication tools to connect with guests directly on WhatsApp:

* Booking confirmations
* Notifications
* Guest queries
* Automated updates

### **5. Housekeeping Management**

A task coordination system to streamline:

* Room cleaning schedules
* Staff task assignments
* Room status updates
* Inspection and turnaround tracking

### **6. Booking Engine**

A direct booking tool designed for the hotel’s own website:

* Real-time rates
* Instant confirmations
* Direct payments
  This feature helps reduce reliance on third-party platforms.

### **7. Payment Gateway Integration**

Support for secure online transactions with:

* Digital payments
* Automated receipts
* Payment tracking
